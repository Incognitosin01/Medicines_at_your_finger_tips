Applies to methionine: oral capsule, oral powder, oral tablet

Side effects not requiring immediate medical attention

Some side effects of methionine may occur that usually do not need medical attention. These side effects may go away during treatment as your body adjusts to the medicine. Also, your health care professional may be able to tell you about ways to prevent or reduce some of these side effects.

Check with your health care professional if any of the following side effects continue or are bothersome or if you have any questions about them:

More common

  • Drowsiness
  • nausea and vomiting
