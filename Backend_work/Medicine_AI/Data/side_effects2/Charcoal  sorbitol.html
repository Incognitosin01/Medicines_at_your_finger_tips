Applies to charcoal/sorbitol: oral suspension

Gastrointestinal

Frequency not reported: Fecal discoloration, vomiting, diarrhea, gastrointestinal obstruction[Ref]

Metabolic

Frequency not reported: Dehydration, electrolyte imbalance, severe hypernatremic dehydration (with use of excessive amounts in pediatric patients)[Ref]

Respiratory

Frequency not reported: Aspiration producing airway obstruction and fatalities, bronchiolitis resulting in death after aspiration of activated charcoal[Ref]

1. "Product Information. Actidose Plus Sorbitol (charcoal-sorbitol)." Perrigo, L. Company, Allegan, MI.
