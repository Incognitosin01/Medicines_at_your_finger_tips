Eczemin Side Effects

Generic Name: pramoxine topical

  • Consumer
  • Professional

Note: This document contains side effect information about pramoxine topical. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Eczemin.

For the Consumer

Applies to pramoxine topical: topical aerosol, topical cream, topical lotion, topical ointment

Side effects include:

Local burning or stinging.

For Healthcare Professionals

Applies to pramoxine topical: compounding powder, rectal foam, topical cream, topical gel, topical kit, topical lotion, topical pad, topical spray, topical stick

General

In general, side effects associated with pramoxine topical (the active ingredient contained in Eczemin) have been reported rarely.[Ref]
