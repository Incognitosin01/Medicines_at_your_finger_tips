Note: This document contains side effect information about bacitracin ophthalmic. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name AK-Tracin.

Applies to bacitracin ophthalmic: ophthalmic ointment

Side effects include:

Local irritation and allergic reactions (pruritus, edema of the conjunctiva and eyelid, conjunctival erythema).
