Applies to iodine topical: topical ointment, topical tincture

Side effects requiring immediate medical attention

Along with its needed effects, iodine topical (the active ingredient contained in Iodine Tincture) may cause some unwanted effects. Although not all of these side effects may occur, if they do occur they may need medical attention.

Check with your doctor as soon as possible if any of the following side effects occur while taking iodine topical:

Rare

  • Blistering, crusting, irritation, itching, or reddening of skin

Symptoms of overdose (when swallowed)

  • Abdominal or stomach pain
  • diarrhea
  • fever
  • nausea
  • not being able to pass urine
  • thirst, severe
  • vomiting
