Note: This document contains side effect information about magnesium amino acids chelate. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name MG Plus Protein.

Applies to magnesium amino acids chelate: oral capsule, oral tablet

Warning

Use exactly as directed on the label, or as prescribed by your doctor. Do not use in larger or smaller amounts or for longer than recommended.

Get emergency medical help if you have signs of an allergic reaction: hives; severe dizziness; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.

Stop using this product and call your doctor at once if you have:

  • severe or ongoing diarrhea;

  • muscle cramps; or

  • depression, feeling tired or irritable.
