Note: This document contains side effect information about budesonide / formoterol / glycopyrrolate. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Breztri Aerosphere.

For the Consumer

Applies to budesonide/formoterol/glycopyrrolate: inhalation aerosol

Warning

Budesonide, formoterol, and glycopyrrolate is not a rescue medicine for bronchospasm attacks. Use only fast-acting inhalation medicine for an attack. Seek medical attention if your breathing problems get worse quickly, or if you think your medications are not working as well.

Get emergency medical help if you have signs of an allergic reaction: hives; difficulty breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.

Call your doctor at once if you have:

  • worsened breathing problems;

  • wheezing, choking, or other breathing problems after using this medication;

  • tremors, nervousness, chest pain, fast or pounding heartbeats;

  • sores or white patches in your mouth and throat, pain when swallowing;

  • flu symptoms, body aches, unusual tiredness;

  • painful or burning when you urinate, little or no urination;

  • blurred vision, tunnel vision, eye pain or redness, or seeing halos around lights;

  • low potassium level--leg cramps, constipation, irregular heartbeats, fluttering in your chest, increased thirst or urination, numbness or tingling, muscle weakness or limp feeling;

  • signs of a lung infection--fever, chills, cough with mucus, chest pain, feeling short of breath; or

  • signs of a hormonal disorder--tiredness or weakness, feeling light-headed, nausea, vomiting.

Common side effects may include:

  • throat pain or irritation;

  • white patches in your mouth or throat;

  • headache, joint pain, muscle spasms;

  • pounding heartbeats, feeling anxious;

  • high blood sugar levels;

  • painful or difficult urination;

  • nausea, diarrhea;

  • cough, hoarse voice;

  • flu symptoms; or

  • cold symptoms such as stuffy or runny nose, sneezing, sinus pain, sore throat.

For Healthcare Professionals

Applies to budesonide/formoterol/glycopyrrolate: inhalation aerosol

Immunologic

Common (1% to 10%): Upper respiratory tract infection, pneumonia, oral candidiasis, influenza, urinary tract infection, sinusitis

Frequency not reported: Candida albicans infection, increased risk of pneumonia in COPD, immunosuppression and risk of infections[Ref]

Respiratory

Common (1% to 10%): Cough

Frequency not reported: Paradoxical bronchospasm, serious asthma-related events (hospitalization, intubation, death)[Ref]

Cardiovascular

Frequency not reported: Cardiovascular effects (increases in pulse rate, systolic or diastolic blood pressure; cardiac arrhythmias such as supraventricular tachycardia and extrasystoles), palpitations, angina pectoris, tachycardia, chest pain[Ref]

Endocrine

Frequency not reported: Hypercorticism and adrenal suppression, sign or symptoms of systemic glucocorticoid steroid effects (e.g. hypofunctional adrenal gland)[Ref]

Ocular

Uncommon (0.1% to 1%): Cataracts

Frequency not reported: Worsening of narrow-angle glaucoma and cataracts, glaucoma, increased intraocular pressure[Ref]

Hypersensitivity

Frequency not reported: Hypersensitivity reactions including anaphylaxis[Ref]

Musculoskeletal

Common (1% to 10%): Back pain, muscle spasms

Frequency not reported: Reduction in bone marrow density[Ref]

Psychiatric

Common (1% to 10%): Dysphonia

Frequency not reported: Anxiety, insomnia, depression, agitation, restlessness, nervousness, abnormal behavior[Ref]

Gastrointestinal

Common (1% to 10%): Diarrhea

Frequency not reported: Dry mouth, throat irritation[Ref]

Genitourinary

Frequency not reported: Worsening of urinary retention, urinary retention

Nervous system

Frequency not reported: Headache, tremor, dizziness[Ref]

Metabolic

Frequency not reported: Hyperglycemia[Ref]

Dermatologic

Frequency not reported: Bruising[Ref]
