Note: This document contains side effect information about abametapir topical. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Xeglyze.

Applies to abametapir topical: topical lotion

Dermatologic

Common (1% to 10%): Erythema, rash, skin burning sensation, contact dermatitis, hair color changes[Ref]

Local

Common (1% to 10%): Scalp erythema/edema, scalp pruritus[Ref]

Ocular

Common (1% to 10%): Eye irritation[Ref]

Gastrointestinal

Common (1% to 10%): Vomiting[Ref]
