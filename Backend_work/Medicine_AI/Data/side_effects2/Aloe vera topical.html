Applies to aloe vera topical: mucous membrane powder for reconstitution, topical cream, topical gel, topical liquid, topical lotion, topical soap, topical spray

Warning

Follow all directions on the product label and package. Tell each of your healthcare providers about all your medical conditions, allergies, and all medicines you use.

Get emergency medical help if you have any of these signs of an allergic reaction while taking aloe vera topical: hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.
