Note: This document contains side effect information about magaldrate / simethicone. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Lowsium Plus.

Applies to magaldrate/simethicone: oral suspension, oral tablet chewable

Musculoskeletal

Musculoskeletal side effects including osteomalacia, bone pain, and muscular weakness have been reported with the use of magaldrate.[Ref]

Other

Other side effects have been reported with the use of magaldrate including malaise, decreased fluoride absorption, milk-alkali syndrome, and aluminum accumulation in serum, bone and the CNS.[Ref]

Nervous system

Nervous system side effects including neurotoxicity and encephalopathy have been reported with the use of magaldrate.[Ref]

Gastrointestinal

Gastrointestinal side effects including rebound hyperacidity, diarrhea, intestinal obstruction, and constipation have been reported with the use of magaldrate.[Ref]

General

Simethicone has no known side effects.[Ref]
