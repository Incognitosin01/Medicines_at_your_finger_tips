Neomycin topical Side Effects

For the Consumer

Applies to neomycin topical: bladder irrigation solution concentrate for irrigation, topical ointments and creams

Side effects include:

Topical: Allergic contact dermatitis.

Bladder irrigation: Irritation of urinary bladder mucosa.

For Healthcare Professionals

Applies to neomycin topical: topical cream, topical ointment

Dermatologic

Frequency not reported: Itching, rash, swelling[Ref]
