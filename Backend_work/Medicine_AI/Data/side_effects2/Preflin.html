Note: This document contains side effect information about steviocalcitriol. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Preflin.

Applies to steviocalcitriol: oral capsule

General

Common reactions may include anorexia, weight loss, weakness, fatigue, disorientation,

vomiting, dehydration, polyuria, constipation, fever, chills, and abdominal pain.[Ref]

Gastrointestinal

Frequency not reported: Constipation, abdominal pain, vomiting[Ref]

Genitourinary

Frequency not reported: polyuria[Ref]

Metabolic

Frequency not reported: Weight loss, anorexia, dehydration[Ref]

Nervous system

Frequency not reported: Disorientation[Ref]

Other

Frequency not reported: Fatigue weakness, fever, chills[Ref]
