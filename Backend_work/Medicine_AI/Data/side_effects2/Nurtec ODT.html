Note: This document contains side effect information about rimegepant. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Nurtec ODT.

Applies to rimegepant: oral tablet disintegrating

General

The most commonly reported adverse reaction has been nausea.[Ref]

Hypersensitivity

Uncommon (Less than 1%): Hypersensitivity reactions

Hypersensitivity reactions including dyspnea and severe rash occurred in less than 1% of patients. Delayed serious hypersensitivity has occurred.

Gastrointestinal

Common (1% to 10%): Nausea
