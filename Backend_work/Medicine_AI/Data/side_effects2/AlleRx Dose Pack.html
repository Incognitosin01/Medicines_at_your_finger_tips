Note: This document contains side effect information about chlorpheniramine / methscopolamine / phenylephrine / pseudoephedrine. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name AlleRx Dose Pack.

Applies to chlorpheniramine/methscopolamine/phenylephrine/pseudoephedrine: oral kit

Cardiovascular

Cardiovascular side effects have included tachycardia, palpitations, flushing, arrhythmias, and cardiovascular collapse with hypotension.[Ref]

Nervous system

Nervous system side effects have included lassitude, drowsiness, giddiness, increased irritability or excitement, headache, fear, anxiety, nervousness,dizziness, restlessness, tremor, weakness, insomnia, hallucinations, convulsions, and CNS depression.[Ref]

Ocular

Ocular side effects have included blurred vision.[Ref]

Gastrointestinal

Gastrointestinal side effects have included nausea and excessive dryness of the nose, throat, and mouth.[Ref]

Respiratory

Respiratory side effects have included respiratory difficulty.[Ref]

Genitourinary

Genitourinary side effects have included dysuria and urinary retention in patients with prostatic hypertrophy.[Ref]
