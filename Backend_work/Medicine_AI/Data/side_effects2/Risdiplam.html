For the Consumer

Applies to risdiplam: oral liquid

Warning

Follow all directions on your medicine label and package. Tell each of your healthcare providers about all your medical conditions, allergies, and all medicines you use.

Get emergency medical help if you have signs of an allergic reaction: hives; difficult breathing; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.

Call your doctor at once if you have:

  • pain or burning when you urinate; or

  • signs of a lung infection--fever, cough with mucus, chest pain, feeling short of breath.

For Healthcare Professionals

Applies to risdiplam: oral liquid

General

The most reported adverse events have included fever, diarrhea, and rash; additionally, among patients with infantile-onset spinal muscular atrophy, upper respiratory tract infection, pneumonia, constipation, and vomiting were reported.[Ref]

Gastrointestinal

Very common (10% or more): Diarrhea (17%), constipation, vomiting

Common (1% to 10%): Mouth and aphthous ulcers

Other

Very common (10% or more): Fever (22%)

Dermatologic

Very common (10% or more): Rash (17%)

Rash includes reports of rash, erythema, rash maculo-papular, rash erythematous, rash popular, dermatitis allergic, and folliculitis.

Respiratory

Respiratory tract infection includes reports of nasopharyngitis, rhinitis, and respiratory tract infection.

Very common (10% or more): Upper respiratory tract infection, pneumonia

Musculoskeletal

Common (1% to 10%): Arthralgia

Genitourinary

Common (1% to 10%): Urinary tract infection

1. "Product Information. Evrysdi (risdiplam)." Genentech, South San Francisco, CA.
