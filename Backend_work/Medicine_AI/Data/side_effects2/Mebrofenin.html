For the Consumer

Applies to mebrofenin: intravenous kit

Side effects requiring immediate medical attention

Along with its needed effects, mebrofenin may cause some unwanted effects. Although not all of these side effects may occur, if they do occur they may need medical attention.

Check with your doctor or nurse immediately if any of the following side effects occur while taking mebrofenin:

Rare

  • Chills
  • hives or welts, itching, or skin rash
  • nausea
  • redness of the skin

Managing side effects (general information)

For Healthcare Professionals

Applies to mebrofenin: intravenous powder for injection

Dermatologic

Rare (less than 0.1%): Urticaria, rash[Ref]

Gastrointestinal

Rare (less than 0.1%): Nausea[Ref]

Other

Rare (less than 0.1%): Chills

Frequency not reported: Death[Ref]
