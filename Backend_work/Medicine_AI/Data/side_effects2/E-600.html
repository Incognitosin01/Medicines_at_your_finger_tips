E-600 Side Effects

Generic Name: vitamin e

  • Consumer
  • Professional

Note: This document contains side effect information about vitamin e. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name E-600.

For the Consumer

Applies to vitamin e: oral capsule, oral capsule liquid filled, oral liquid, oral oil, oral solution, oral tablet, oral tablet chewable

Side effects requiring immediate medical attention

Along with its needed effects, vitamin e (the active ingredient contained in E-600) may cause some unwanted effects. Although not all of these side effects may occur, if they do occur they may need medical attention.

Check with your doctor as soon as possible if any of the following side effects occur while taking vitamin e:

With doses greater than 400 Units a day and long-term use

  • Blurred vision
  • diarrhea
  • dizziness
  • headache
  • nausea or stomach cramps
  • unusual tiredness or weakness

Managing side effects (general information)

Applies to vitamin e: compounding powder, oral capsule, oral liquid, oral solution, oral tablet, oral tablet chewable, oral and topical oil
