Note: This document contains side effect information about dextran, high molecular weight. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Hyskon.

Applies to dextran, high molecular weight: intrauterine solution, intravenous solution

Warning

You should not receive this medication if you have severe kidney disease, severe congestive heart failure, or uncontrolled bleeding.

Get emergency medical help if you have any of these signs of an allergic reaction while taking dextran, high molecular weight (the active ingredient contained in Hyskon) hives or skin rash; stuffy nose, wheezing, chest tightness; feeling like you might pass out; swelling of your face, lips, tongue, or throat.

Tell your caregiver right away if you have:

  • weak or shallow breathing; or

  • a light-headed feeling, like you might pass out.

  • nausea, vomiting;
