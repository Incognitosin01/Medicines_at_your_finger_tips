Zilactin-L Side Effects

Generic Name: benzyl alcohol topical

  • Managing Side Effects

Note: This document contains side effect information about benzyl alcohol topical. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Zilactin-L.

Applies to benzyl alcohol topical: topical lotion

Side effects include:

Pruritus, erythema, pyoderma, ocular irritation, application site symptoms (irritation, anesthesia, hypoesthesia).
