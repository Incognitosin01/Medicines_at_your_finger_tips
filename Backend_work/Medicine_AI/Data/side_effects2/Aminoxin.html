Aminoxin Side Effects

Generic Name: pyridoxine

  • Consumer
  • Professional

Note: This document contains side effect information about pyridoxine. Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Aminoxin.

For the Consumer

Applies to pyridoxine: capsule, powder, solution, tablet, tablet extended release

Side effects requiring immediate medical attention

Along with its needed effects, pyridoxine (the active ingredient contained in Aminoxin) may cause some unwanted effects. Although not all of these side effects may occur, if they do occur they may need medical attention.

Check with your doctor as soon as possible if any of the following side effects occur while taking pyridoxine:

With large doses

  • Clumsiness
  • numbness of hands or feet

Managing side effects (general information)

For Healthcare Professionals

Applies to pyridoxine: compounding powder, injectable solution, oral capsule, oral tablet

Nervous system

The profound sensory loss following treatment with high doses of parenteral pyridoxine (the active ingredient contained in Aminoxin) may not be reversible.[Ref]

Frequency not reported: Paresthesia, somnolence[Ref]

Metabolic

Frequency not reported: Low serum folic acid levels[Ref]
