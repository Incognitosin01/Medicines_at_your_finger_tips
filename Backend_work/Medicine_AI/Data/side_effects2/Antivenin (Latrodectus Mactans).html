Antivenin (Latrodectus Mactans) Side Effects

Generic Name: antivenin (black widow spider)

  • Consumer
  • Professional

Note: This document contains side effect information about antivenin (black widow spider). Some of the dosage forms listed on this page may not apply to the brand name Antivenin (Latrodectus Mactans).

For the Consumer

Applies to antivenin (black widow spider): parenteral powder for injection

Side effects include:

Sensitivity reactions, muscle cramps.

For Healthcare Professionals

Applies to antivenin (black widow spider): injectable powder for injection

Hypersensitivity

Frequency not reported: Hypersensitivity reactions including anaphylaxis and serum sickness[Ref]

Musculoskeletal

Frequency not reported: Muscle cramps
