Cemiplimab-rwlc Pharmacokinetics

Absorption

Pharmacokinetics are linear and dose proportional over dosage range of 1–10 mg/kg IV every 2 weeks and at a dosage of 350 mg IV every 3 weeks.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Steady-state concentrations achieved by approximately 4 months.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Systemic exposure not affected by total bilirubin concentrations of 0.02–2.63 mg/dL or Clcr ≥25 mL/minute.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>
