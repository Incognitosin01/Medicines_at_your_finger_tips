USES

  • Helps prevent sunburns
  • Higher SPF gives more sunburn protection
  • Apply liberally as needed 15 to 20 minutes before sun exposure
  • Reapply as needed or after swimming, perspiring or towel drying
  • Children under 6 months of age: ask doctor

WARNINGS

For external use only. When using this product keep out of eyes. Rinse with water to remove. Stop use and ask a doctor if rash or irritation develops and lasts. Keep out of reach of children. If swallowed, get medical help or contact Poison Center right away.
