INDICATIONS & USAGE

  • Treats most common bird respiratory ailments

  • Special formula helps correct vitamin deficiencies and diarrhea during treatment
  • Readily soluble and easily administered in bird's drinking water

INSTRUCTIONS

Precautions

Read instructions before using. For pet bird use only. Not for human consumption. Keep stock solution covered in a refrigerated dark location, and discard after five days.
