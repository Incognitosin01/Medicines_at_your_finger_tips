Uses for Tazarotene

Psoriasis

Treatment of stable plaque psoriasis with ≤20% body surface area involvement.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>4</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>16</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>19</sup></body></html>

Combination therapy with a mid-or high-potency topical corticosteroid more effective than either agent alone.<html><head></head><body><sup>21</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>22</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>23</sup></body></html>

0.1% cream or gel more effective in improving psoriasis manifestations;<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>19</sup></body></html> 0.05% cream or gel associated with less local irritation.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>4</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>17</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>19</sup></body></html>

Acne

Treatment of mild to moderate acne vulgaris.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>7</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>19</sup></body></html>

Efficacy in patients resistant to oral antibiotics or previously treated with other retinoids not established.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Photoaging

Palliative therapy to improve dermatologic changes (e.g., fine wrinkling, mottled hypo- or hyperpigmentation, benign lentigines) associated with photodamage.<html><head></head><body><sup>27</sup></body></html>

Use as an adjunct to a comprehensive skin care plan and sun avoidance program.<html><head></head><body><sup>27</sup></body></html> (See Administration under Dosage and Administration.)

Does not eliminate or prevent wrinkles, repair photodamaged skin, reverse photoaging, or restore youthful or younger dermal histologic pattern.<html><head></head><body><sup>27</sup></body></html> No mitigating effects on severe manifestations of chronic sunlight exposure (e.g., coarse/deep wrinkling, tactile roughness, telangiectasia, skin laxity, keratinocytic atypia, melanocytic atypia, dermal elastosis) demonstrated.<html><head></head><body><sup>27</sup></body></html>

Safety and efficacy not established for use in nonwhite (e.g., Asian, Hispanic) patients or Fitzpatrick skin types V and VI.<html><head></head><body><sup>27</sup></body></html>
