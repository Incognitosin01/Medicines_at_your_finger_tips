Treatment of endocarditis† caused by viridans streptococci (e.g., S. milleri, S. mitis, S. mutans) or S. bovis (nonenterococcal group D streptococcus); used as an adjunct to recommended anti-infectives (e.g., penicillin G, ceftriaxone, vancomycin).<html><head></head><body><sup>k</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>l</sup></body></html>
