  • Laxative, Osmotic

Use: Labeled Indications

Colon cleansing prior to colonoscopy: For the cleansing of the colon as a preparation for colonoscopy in adults.

Contraindications

GI obstruction or ileus; bowel perforation; toxic colitis or toxic megacolon; gastric retention.
