Introduction

Antisense oligonucleotide that binds to the pre-mRNA of survival motor neuron 2 (SMN2).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>4</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>8</sup></body></html>

Uses for Nusinersen

Spinal Muscular Atrophy

Management of spinal muscular atrophy (designated an orphan drug by FDA for use in this condition).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html>

Associated with substantial improvement in motor milestones (e.g., ability to sit unassisted, stand, walk).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

May be used across full range of patients with spinal muscular atrophy (e.g., infantile-onset to later-onset disease).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html> Available evidence to date supports early initiation of therapy.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html>

Administration

Administer by intrathecal injection only.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Administer by, or under the direction of, a clinician experienced in performing lumbar punctures.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> For specific procedures and techniques of administration, consult manufacturer's full prescribing information.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Prior to administration, allow vials to warm to room temperature; do not use external heat sources for warming.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Solution should be clear and colorless; do not use if visible particulates or discoloration observed.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Prior to administration, remove 5 mL of CSF.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> To prepare dose for intrathecal injection, withdraw 5 mL (12 mg) of nusinersen injection solution into a syringe using proper aseptic technique; administer by direct (“bolus”) intrathecal injection over 1–3 minutes using a spinal anesthesia needle.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Do not inject into areas of skin showing signs of infection or inflammation.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Administer drug within 4 hours after removal from vial.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Vials contain no preservatives and are intended for single use only; discard unused contents.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

If a loading dose is delayed or missed, administer delayed or missed dose as soon as possible with at least 14 days between doses; resume regular dosing schedule thereafter.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> If a maintenance dose is delayed or missed, administer delayed or missed dose as soon as possible and resume regular dosing schedule.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Dosage

Dosage of nusinersen sodium expressed in terms of nusinersen.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Initiate therapy with a series of 4 loading doses of 12 mg (5 mL) each as follows: administer first 3 doses every 14 days and the fourth dose 30 days after the third dose.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Thereafter, continue with a maintenance dosage of 12 mg once every 4 months.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Warnings/Precautions

Thrombocytopenia (sometimes severe and acute) and coagulation abnormalities observed following administration of some antisense oligonucleotides.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Possible increased risk of bleeding complications.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Monitor platelet count, PT, and aPTT at baseline, prior to each dose of the drug, and as clinically indicated.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Renal toxicity, including potentially fatal glomerulonephritis, observed following administration of some antisense oligonucleotides.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Nusinersen accumulates in the kidneys; proteinuria has been reported.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html>

Perform quantitative spot urine protein testing (preferably using first morning urine specimen) at baseline, prior to each dose of the drug, and as clinically indicated.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> For urine protein concentrations >0.2 g/L, consider repeat testing and further evaluation.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Development of treatment-emergent antibodies to nusinersen reported.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html> Data insufficient to determine whether these antibodies affect clinical response to or adverse effects or pharmacokinetic profile of the drug.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

No adequate data on use in pregnant women.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Not known whether nusinersen is distributed into human milk, affects milk production, or affects the breast-fed infant.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Consider known benefits of breast-feeding along with woman's clinical need for nusinersen and any potential adverse effects of the drug or disease on the infant.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Safety and efficacy established in pediatric patients of all ages.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

No experience in geriatric patients; spinal muscular atrophy is generally a disease of children and young adults.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Not expected to affect nusinersen exposure because of intrathecal route of administration.<html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html>

Not expected to affect nusinersen exposure because of intrathecal route of administration.<html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html>

Nusinersen Pharmacokinetics

Absorption

Following intrathecal administration, trough plasma concentrations relatively low compared with trough CSF concentrations.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Following intrathecal administration, mean peak plasma concentrations and AUC increased approximately dose proportionally up to dose of 12 mg.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Peak plasma concentrations achieved in a median of 1.7–6 hours.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

No apparent correlations between age or total body weight and CSF concentrations.<html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html>

Distribution

Intrathecally-administered nusinersen appears to rapidly distribute from CSF into systemic circulation, consistent with normal CSF turnover.<html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>4</sup></body></html> Intrathecal injection into CSF allows distribution into target CNS tissues (e.g., motor neurons).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>4</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>7</sup></body></html> Also distributed within peripheral tissues (e.g., skeletal muscle, liver, kidney).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>4</sup></body></html>

Does not cross intact blood-brain barrier when administered systemically.<html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html>

Not known whether distributed into human milk.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Elimination

Exonuclease-mediated hydrolysis.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Principally excreted in urine.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

At 24 hours post-dose, only 0.5% of administered dose recovered in urine.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

In CSF: 135–177 days.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html>

In plasma: 63–87 days.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Stability

Storage

2–8°C.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Store unopened vials in original carton to protect from light.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Do not freeze.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

If refrigeration not possible, may store at ≤30°C in the original carton for ≤14 days.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Unopened vials may be moved to and from refrigerated storage if necessary; if removed from original carton, total combined time out of refrigeration should not exceed 30 hours at a temperature that does not exceed 25°C.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>
