Alternative to penicillin G or ampicillin for prevention of perinatal group B streptococcal (GBS) disease† (early-onset neonatal GBS disease) in penicillin-allergic pregnant women who do not have immediate-type penicillin hypersensitivity (see Cross-hypersensitivity under Cautions).<html><head></head><body><sup>111</sup></body></html>
