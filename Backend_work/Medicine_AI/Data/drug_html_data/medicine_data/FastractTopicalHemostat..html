FasTRACT™ topical hemostat is an increased activity coagulative hemostatic gel containing 10% ferric sulfate, built around a novel organic polymer. This unique formulation is easy to use and rinses clean. FasTRACT hemostatic gel provides superior hemostasis at a much lower concentration of ferric sulfate. Because it rinses cleanly, FasTRACT minimizes post-operative black discoloration under restorations.

  • Not for use in patients with known sensitivity or allergies to iron or iron salts.
  • Not for use in pulpectomy procedures.
  • Ensure the material has had time to react with the blood and exposed capillaries before proceeding with subsequent procedures.
  • Do not store above 100°F (38°C). Do not freeze. Do not use beyond indicated shelf life.
  • Do not use if the packaging has been damaged, or if the seals are found to be broken or open.
  • Shelf Life: 2 years

Twist on scrub tip brush for easy application. Deliver hemostatic gel directly to bleeding areas. Use the fiber-flocked ball to burnish/rub gel into bleeding capillaries. Continue to burnish until bleeding stops. When control of bleeding is achieved, use suction and heavy air/water spray to remove coagulum and residual gel from surface. If bleeding restarts, repeat application and burnishing.
