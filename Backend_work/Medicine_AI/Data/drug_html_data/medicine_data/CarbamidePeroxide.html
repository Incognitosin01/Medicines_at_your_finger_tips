Introduction

Source of hydrogen peroxide and nascent oxygen; release of oxygen results in mild mechanical effervescence.<html><head></head><body><sup>a</sup></body></html>

Uses for Carbamide Peroxide

Cerumen Removal

Occasional aid to soften, loosen, and remove excessive cerumen (earwax) from external ear canal.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>4</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>5</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html>

Oral Irritation and Inflammation

Oral debriding agent and oral wound cleanser for topical treatment of canker sores (aphthous ulcers) and minor wounds or gum inflammation resulting from minor dental procedures, dentures, orthodontic appliances, accidental injury, or other irritations of the mouth and gums.<html><head></head><body><sup>7</sup></body></html>

Aid in removal of phlegm, mucus, or other secretions associated with occasional sore mouth.<html><head></head><body><sup>7</sup></body></html>

Adjunct to oral hygiene when normal cleansing measures are inadequate or impossible (e.g., total-care geriatric patients) or adjunct to oral hygiene after regular brushing.<html><head></head><body><sup>a</sup></body></html>

Actions

  • Exposure of carbamide peroxide to moisture (e.g., on skin, in mouth) slowly releases hydrogen peroxide and oxygen; effervescence from release of oxygen mechanically removes debris from inaccessible regions.<html><head></head><body><sup>a</sup></body></html> Release of oxygen also results in weak antibacterial properties.<html><head></head><body><sup>a</sup></body></html>

  • For otic use, penetrates and softens cerumen (earwax) and release of oxygen provides mild mechanical action resulting in loosening of softened cerumen.<html><head></head><body><sup>a</sup></body></html>

  • Probably inhibits odor-causing bacteria, resulting in deodorant activity.<html><head></head><body><sup>a</sup></body></html>
