Tagraxofusp-erzs Pharmacokinetics

Absorption

AUC and peak plasma concentrations reduced by 34.6 and 50.6%, respectively, in patients with preexisting anti-tagraxofusp antibodies.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>7</sup></body></html>

Mild (total bilirubin concentration not exceeding the ULN with AST concentration exceeding the ULN, or total bilirubin concentration exceeding the ULN, but ≤1.5 times the ULN, with any AST concentration) or moderate (total bilirubin concentration >1.5 times but ≤3 times the ULN, with any AST concentration) hepatic impairment: No effect on pharmacokinetics of tagraxofusp-erzs.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Severe hepatic impairment (total bilirubin concentration >3 times the ULN with any AST concentration): Not studied.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Mild or moderate renal impairment (eGFR 30–89 mL/minute per 1.73 m2): No effect on pharmacokinetics of tagraxofusp-erzs.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Severe renal impairment (eGFR 15–29 mL/minute per 1.73 m2): Not studied.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Clearance increased by 1.96-fold in patients with preexisting anti-tagraxofusp antibodies.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>7</sup></body></html>
