Agri Labs Ob Lube Gallon

For Animal Use Only

USE: Can be used as a lubricant of operator's hands and arms in obstetrical work, vaginal examinations, and rectal examinations; may be used as a lubricant for catheters and stomach tubes.

DIRECTIONS: Apply o b lube to gloves or sleeves when performing gynecological procedures and rectal examinations. After use, rinse thoroughly with water to remove excess from exposed surfaces or animal.

May be used with or without water.

Use on large or small animals.
