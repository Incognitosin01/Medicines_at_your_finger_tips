DIRECTIONS: For oral mucosa. Use only as directed by a dentist.

CAUTION: Keep out of reach of children. For professional use only.

Each gram of 20 percent benzocaine gel contains 200mg of benzocaine in a water soluble base of:

  • FDC Blue 1
  • FDC Red 40
  • FDC Yellow 5 (tartrazine) as a color additive
  • FDC Yellow 6
  • Flavor
  • PEG 400
  • PEG 3350
  • Sodium Saccharin
  • Store at controlled room temperature 59-86 F (15-30 C)
  • Protect from freezing and heat
