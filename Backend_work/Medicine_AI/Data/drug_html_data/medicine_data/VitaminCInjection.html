INDICATIONS

For use as a nutritive supplement of vitamin C in cattle, horses, sheep, swine, dogs and cats.

Precautions

Since pressure may develop on long storage, precautions should be taken to release pressure before use. Storage under refrigeration will reduce possibility of pressure build-up.

Vitamin C Injection Dosage and Administration

Administer intramuscularly 1 to 10 mL, depending on condition, species, and body weight. Repeat daliy or as indicated by desired response.
