STERILE - MULTIPLE DOSE VIAL
An injectable source of the volatile salts of Pitcher Plant (Sarraceniaceae) with 5% w/v Ethanol as a solution aide and
0.75% w/v Benzyl Alcohol as a preservative.

USAGE: P-Bloc™ sterile aqueous solution is indicated for the temporary relief of symptoms
associated with neurologic pain.

DOSAGE AND ADMINISTRATION: Paravertebral injections should be observed as follows: Local infiltration 5-10 mL,
Caudal Canal 10 mL, Sciatic Nerve 10 mL, Cervical 2-3 mL, Dorsal 5-10 mL, Lumbar 5-10 mL, Sacral 3-5 mL.

CAUTION: Caution should be exercised during injection. Procedure should be done slowly. P-Bloc™ should not be
used in areas of local inflammation. Keep out of reach of children.

WARNINGS: There may appear localized and usually transient side effects resulting in a heat sensation or aggravated
symptoms. Withdraw plunger of syringe to make sure the needle point is not in a blood vessel. Injection should be made through
a procaine wheal.

CONTRAINDICATIONS: P-Bloc™ is contraindicated in areas of local inflammation.

Store between 15°and 30°C (59° and 86°F).
