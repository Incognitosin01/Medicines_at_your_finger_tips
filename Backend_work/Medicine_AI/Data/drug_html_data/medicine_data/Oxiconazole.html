Oxiconazole Pharmacokinetics

Absorption

Only low concentrations absorbed systemically following topical application to skin.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Distribution

Topical application to skin results in highest concentrations in epidermis and lower concentrations in upper and deeper corneum.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> In vitro on animal skin, oxiconazole 1% cream was retained in the horny layer of the epidermis for up to 96 hours after topical application.<html><head></head><body><sup>10</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>20</sup></body></html>

Distributed into milk.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>32</sup></body></html>

Not known whether systemically absorbed oxiconazole crosses the placenta.<html><head></head><body><sup>32</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>33</sup></body></html>
