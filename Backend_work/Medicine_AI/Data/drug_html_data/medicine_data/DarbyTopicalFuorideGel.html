Darby 9521547 Grape

60 Second Fluoride Gel

2.72% Topical Sodium Fluoride Gel

1.23% fluoride ions

16.23 fl oz (480 ml)

NDC 66467-2150-1

A topical anti-caries preparation. Optimized low pH for penetration. Ideal thixotropic action. Artificially flavored.

Directions:

1. Following prophylaxis treatment, fill 1/3 of tray with gel

2. Insert tray in the patient's mouth, have patient bite down lightly for 60 seconds

3. Remove tray and have patient expectorate excess

4. Advise patient not to eat, drink or rinse for 30 minutes after the application

Medicinal ingredients:

Fluoride ions 1.23% w/w (from 2.72% w/w sodium fluoride)

Non-Medicinal Ingredients: Carboxy methyl cellulose, phosphoric acid, purified water, red 33, blue 1, saccharin sodium, titanium dioxide, grape flavor

Warnings:

KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN.

For professional use only. Do not swallow.

Not recommended for use by children under 18 months of age.

Store at room temperature. Do not expose to excessive heat over 104°F (40°C).

Do not use if seal is broken.
