Active Ingredient

Each 1 g piece contains 90 mg of petrolatum.

Manufactured by: Nestlé Purina PetCare Company, St. Louis, MO 63164 USA

DIRECTIONS FOR USE

Do not feed to cats under 6 months of age.

Feed recommended amount all at one time.

TO HELP ELIMINATE HAIRBALLS IN ADULT CATS

Feed 3 to 6 pieces daily until the signs disappear.

TO HELP PREVENT HAIRBALLS IN ADULT CATS

Feed 3 to 6 pieces once or twice per week.

Warning

Use only as directed. Should vomiting persist, consult a veterinarian. Do not administer to cats less than 6 months of age. Keep this and all other medication out of the reach of children and pets.

Remember that this product is not a substitute for a balanced meal. A proper diet of a complete and balanced food, fresh water in a clean container, exercise, and regular veterinary visits are all necessary to keep your cat in good health.

STORAGE

Keep package closed. Store at room temperature away from heat or direct sunlight.
