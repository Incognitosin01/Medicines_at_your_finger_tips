Use and store only outdoors or in a well-ventilated place. Use a back flow preventive device in the piping. Use only with equipment rated for the cylinder pressure. Close valve after each use and when empty. Protect from sunlight when ambient temperature exceeds 52 C (125 F). Read and follow the Safety Data Sheet (SDS) before use. FIRST AID: IF ACCIDENTALLY INHALED: Remove person to fresh air and keep comfortable for breathing. Get medical advice/attention.
