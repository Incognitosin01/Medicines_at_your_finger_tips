The Prenatabs FA brand name has been discontinued in the U.S. If generic versions of this product have been approved by the FDA, there may be generic equivalents available.

Each tablet contains:



Vitamin C (as ascorbic acid).....120 mg

Vitamin D (D3).....400 IU

Vitamin E (dl-alpha tocopheryl acetate)....30 IU

Vitamin B1 (thiamine mononitrate).....3 mg

Vitamin B2 (riboflavin).....3 mg

Niacin (niacinamide).....20 mg

Vitamin B6 (pyridoxine HCl).....3 mg

Folic Acid.....1 mg

Vitamin B12 (cyanocobalamin).....8 mcg

Calcium (calcium carbonate).....200 mg

Iron (ferrous fumarate).....29 mg

Iodine (potassium iodide).....150 mcg

Zinc (zinc oxide).....15 mg


Precaution

Folic Acid may obscure pernicious anemia or produce remission while neurologic progress may continue.

Suggested Dosage

One tablet daily or as directed by the physician.

Warning

Accidental overdose of iron-containing product is a leading cause of fatal poisoning in children under six. Keep this product out of reach of children. In case of accidental overdose, call a doctor or poison control center immediately.


Store at room temperature, USP.

Call your doctor for medical advise about side effects. You may report side effects to FDA at 1-800-FDA-1088.
