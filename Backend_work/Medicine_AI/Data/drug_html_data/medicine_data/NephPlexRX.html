Description

NephPlex RX is a prescription folic acid supplement with additional nutrients for kidney dialysis patients. NephPlex RX is a white, film-coated, oval shaped tablet.

Suggested Dosage

One tablet daily or as directed by the physician.

Precaution

Folic Acid may obscure pernicious anemia or produce remission while neurologic progress may continue.
