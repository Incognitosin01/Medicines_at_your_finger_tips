Directions

DIRECTIONS: for the most discriminating felines. A few tasty licks from your finger (or from a silver or crystal dish), will start your cat on a life free from nasty hairballs.

Dosage

DOSAGE: For cats. Start with a 1-1/4 to 2-1/2 inch ribbon (1 half or 1 full teaspoon) for the first 2-3 days.

Lower dose to 3/4 inch ribbon (one third teaspoon) 2-3 times per week.

For kittens over 4 weeks, use half doses.
