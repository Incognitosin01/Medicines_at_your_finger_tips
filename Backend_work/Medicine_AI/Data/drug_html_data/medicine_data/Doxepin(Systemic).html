Warning

  • Antidepressants may increase risk of suicidal thinking and behavior (suicidality) in children, adolescents, and young adults (18–24 years of age) with major depressive disorder and other psychiatric disorders; balance this risk with clinical need.<html><head></head><body><sup>h</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>i</sup></body></html> Doxepin is not approved for use in pediatric patients <12 years of age.<html><head></head><body><sup>a</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>k</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>l</sup></body></html> (See Pediatric Use under Cautions.)
