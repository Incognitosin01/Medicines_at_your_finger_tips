Warnings/Precautions

For topical ophthalmic use only.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

To avoid contamination of the dropper tip and solution, do not to touch the eyelids or surrounding areas with the dropper tip.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Bepotastine Pharmacokinetics

Absorption
