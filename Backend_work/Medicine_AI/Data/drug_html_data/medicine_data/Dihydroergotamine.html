Dihydroergotamine Pharmacokinetics

Absorption

Following oral administration, bioavailability is <1% because of first-pass metabolism.<html><head></head><body><sup>d</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>129</sup></body></html>

Following intranasal administration, mean bioavailability is 32% relative to parenteral administration.<html><head></head><body><sup>129</sup></body></html>

Absolute bioavailability for sub-Q and IM routes has not been determined, however, no difference was observed in bioavailability from IM and sub-Q doses.<html><head></head><body><sup>139</sup></body></html>

Intranasal: About 30 minutes.<html><head></head><body><sup>b</sup></body></html>

IM: 15–30 minutes.<html><head></head><body><sup>d</sup></body></html>

IV: Variable, usually <5 minutes.<html><head></head><body><sup>d</sup></body></html>

Intranasal: At least 4 hours.<html><head></head><body><sup>b</sup></body></html>

Sub-Q or IV: Approximately 8 hours.<html><head></head><body><sup>d</sup></body></html>

Distribution

93%.<html><head></head><body><sup>129</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>139</sup></body></html>

Elimination

Extensively metabolized in the liver to several metabolites; principal metabolite is pharmacologically active.<html><head></head><body><sup>129</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>139</sup></body></html>

Metabolized by CYP3A4.<html><head></head><body><sup>129</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>139</sup></body></html>

Eliminated principally in feces (via bile) as metabolites; <10% of a dose is excreted in urine.<html><head></head><body><sup>129</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>139</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>d</sup></body></html>

Following intranasal administration: Biphasic; terminal half-life is approximately 10 hours.<html><head></head><body><sup>129</sup></body></html>

Following IM or IV administration: Multi-exponential; terminal half-life is approximately 9 hours.<html><head></head><body><sup>139</sup></body></html>

<25°C; do not refrigerate or freeze.<html><head></head><body><sup>129</sup></body></html>
