Uses for Eflornithine

Reduction of Unwanted Facial Hair

Reduction of unwanted facial hair in women.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html>

Administration

Apply to skin as a 13.9% cream.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html>

Apply only to affected areas of the face and adjacent involved areas under the chin; not intended for oral, ophthalmic, or intravaginal use.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Apply a thin film and rub in thoroughly.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Do not wash treated areas for at least 4 hours after application.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html>

Continue hair removal techniques (e.g., plucking, tweezing, cutting, shaving), as needed, throughout treatment.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Do not apply cream for at least 5 minutes after hair removal.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

May apply cosmetic products and sunscreens after cream has dried.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html>

Dosage

Available as eflornithine hydrochloride; dosage expressed in terms of the salt.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Apply a thin film twice daily at least 8 hours apart (or as directed by clinician).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html>

If skin irritation or intolerance develops, reduce application frequency to once daily.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>8</sup></body></html> If irritation continues, discontinue therapy.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>8</sup></body></html>

Eflornithine Pharmacokinetics

Absorption

Following topical application, percutaneous absorption is <1%.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Distribution

Not known whether eflornithine crosses the placenta or is distributed into milk.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

Elimination

No evidence that eflornithine is metabolized.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>b</sup></body></html>

Any absorbed eflornithine is excreted in urine as unchanged drug.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>b</sup></body></html>

Approximately 8 hours at steady state.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>b</sup></body></html>

Stability

Storage

25°C (may be exposed to 15–30°C); do not freeze.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>
