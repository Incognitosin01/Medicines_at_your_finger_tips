INDICATIONS

INDICATIONS: For use as an aid in the management of slow-healing surface wounds on horses and mules.

WARNING: Not for use on horses intended for food.

CAUTION

CAUTION: In case of deep or puncture wounds or serious burns consult veterinarian. If redness, irritation, or swelling persists or increases, discontinue use and consult veterinarian.

DIRECTIONS FOR USE

DIRECTIONS FOR USE: Apply powder freely to wounds twice daily. Repeat as indicated.
