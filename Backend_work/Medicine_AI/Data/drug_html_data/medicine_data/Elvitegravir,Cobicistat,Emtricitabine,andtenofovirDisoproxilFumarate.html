Introduction

Antiretroviral; fixed combination of elvitegravir, cobicistat, emtricitabine, and tenofovir disoproxil fumarate (tenofovir DF) (EVG/c/FTC/TDF).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html> Elvitegravir (EVG) is an HIV integrase strand transfer inhibitor (INSTI) antiretroviral; cobicistat is a CYP3A inhibitor used as a pharmacokinetic enhancer to decrease metabolism and increase plasma concentrations of EVG; emtricitabine (FTC) and tenofovir DF (TDF) are HIV nucleoside reverse transcriptase inhibitors (NRTIs).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html>

Treatment of HIV Infection

Postexposure Prophylaxis following Occupational Exposure to HIV

Postexposure prophylaxis of HIV infection following occupational exposure† (PEP) in health-care personnel and others exposed via percutaneous injury (e.g., needlestick, cut with sharp object) or mucous membrane or nonintact skin (e.g., chapped, abraded, dermatitis) contact with blood, tissue, or other body fluids that might contain HIV.<html><head></head><body><sup>199</sup></body></html>

Advice to Patients

  • Critical nature of compliance with HIV therapy and importance of remaining under the care of a clinician.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Importance of taking as prescribed; do not alter or discontinue antiretroviral regimen without consulting clinician.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

  • Antiretroviral therapy is not a cure for HIV infection;<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> opportunistic infections and other complications associated with HIV disease may still occur.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

  • Advise patients that sustained decreases in plasma HIV RNA have been associated with reduced risk of progression to acquired immunodeficiency syndrome (AIDS) and death.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>

  • Importance of taking EVG/c/FTC/TDF with food.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>
