Spectogard Scour Chek

Generic Name: spectinomycin solution
Dosage Form: FOR ANIMAL USE ONLY

  • Indications and Usage
  • Dosage Forms and Strengths
  • Dosage and Administration

For use in pigs under four (4) weeks of age for treatment and control of porcine enteric colibacillosis (scours) caused by E. coli susceptible to spectinomycin.

EACH mL CONTAINS: Spectinomycin (from spectinomycin dihydrochloride pentahydrate)..... 50mg

RESIDUE WARNING: This product is only intended for use in pigs under four (4) weeks of age or weighing less than 15lbs.

Do not administer within 21 days of slaughter.
