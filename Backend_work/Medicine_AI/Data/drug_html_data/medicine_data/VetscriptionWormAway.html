Vetscription Worm Away Dosage and Administration

For puppies and dogs weighing:

Animals one to five pounds.....1 capsule

Animals six to ten pounds.....2 capsules

Animals eleven to fifteen pounds.....3 capsules

Animals sixteen to twenty pounds.....4 capsules

Animals twenty-one to twenty-five pounds.....5 capsules

Animals over twenty-five pounds.....6 capsules

CAUTION

KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN

RECOMMENDED STORAGE

Store at controlled room temperature 15-30 C (59-86 F)
