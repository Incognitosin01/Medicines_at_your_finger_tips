Warning:

Observe withdrawal time shown in table above. Keep out of reach of children. Avoid contact with skin. Direct contact with skin or mucous membranes may cause irritation.

Caution:

Do not feed undiluted. Do not use in feeds for animals other than swine. The effects of tiamulin on swine reproductive performance, pregnancy and lactation have not been determined.

Contraindications:

Swine being treated with Denagard (tiamulin) should not have access to feeds containing polyether ionophores (e.g., lasalocid, monensin. narasin, salinomycin and semduramicin) as adverse reactions may occur. If signs of toxicity occur, discontinue use.

Restricted Drug (California): Use only as directed.
