Composition

Composition:
Each fluid ounce (2
tablespoons) of Soothables
K'P'A'D, Anti-Diarrheal Liquid
contains:
Kaolin 90 gr (5.8 g)
Pectin 4 gr (0.26 g)
In a palatable base. Flavorings
and color added.

Indication for Use

Kaolin is an anti-diarrheal
medicine additive for the
adsorption of toxins. This
adsorbent action helps relieve
the irritation, discomfort and
cramping associated with
diarrhea.
Pectin is a soluble dietary fiber
naturally present in citrus fruits.

Keep out of reach of children and pets
