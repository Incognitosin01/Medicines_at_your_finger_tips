USE DIRECTIONS:
Do not use in concentrated form. Must
be diluted prior to use.


PROTECT FROM FREEZING
IF FROZEN: SHAKE VIGOROUSLY
AFTER THAWING

DANGER: CONTAINS IODINE AND PHOSPHORIC
ACID. CAUSES SEVERE SKIN IRRITATION AND
EYE DAMAGE.

KEEP OUT OF REACH OF CHILDREN!

FIRST AID:
INTERNAL: If swallowed, do not induce vomiting.
Drink large quantities of water. See physician immediately.

EYES: Flush eyes with clear water for 15 minutes. If
irritated, obtain medical guidance.

GET MEDICAL ATTENTION IMMEDIATELY.
