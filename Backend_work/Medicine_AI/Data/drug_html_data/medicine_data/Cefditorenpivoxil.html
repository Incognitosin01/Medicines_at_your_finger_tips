Uses for cefditoren pivoxil

Cefditoren is used in the treatment of infections caused by bacteria. It works by killing bacteria or preventing their growth.

Cefditoren pivoxil is used to treat some throat and lung infections, including bronchitis and tonsillitis. It is also used to treat some skin infections.

Cefditoren pivoxil is available only with your doctor's prescription.

Before using cefditoren pivoxil

In deciding to use a medicine, the risks of taking the medicine must be weighed against the good it will do. This is a decision you and your doctor will make. For cefditoren pivoxil, the following should be considered:

Allergies

Tell your doctor if you have ever had any unusual or allergic reaction to cefditoren pivoxil or any other medicines. Also tell your health care professional if you have any other types of allergies, such as to foods, dyes, preservatives, or animals. For non-prescription products, read the label or package ingredients carefully.

Pediatric

Studies on cefditoren pivoxil have been done only in adult patients, and there is no specific information comparing use of cefditoren in children less than 12 years of age with use in other age groups.

Geriatric

Cefditoren pivoxil has been tested and has not been shown to cause different side effects or problems in older people than it does in younger adults.

Breastfeeding

Studies in women suggest that this medication poses minimal risk to the infant when used during breastfeeding.

Other medical problems

The presence of other medical problems may affect the use of cefditoren pivoxil. Make sure you tell your doctor if you have any other medical problems, especially:

  • Carnitine deficiency—May be worsened by cefditoren
  • Kidney disease—Cefditoren may need to be given at a lower dose

Proper use of cefditoren pivoxil

To help clear up your infection completely, keep taking cefditoren pivoxil for the full time of treatment, even if you begin to feel better after a few days. Also, if you stop taking cefditoren pivoxil too soon, your symptoms may return.

Cefditoren pivoxil works best when there is a constant amount in the blood or urine. To help keep the amount constant, do not miss any doses. Also, it is best to take the doses at evenly spaced times, day and night. For example, if you are to take four doses a day, the doses should be spaced about 6 hours apart. If this interferes with your sleep or other daily activities, or if you need help in planning the best times to take your medicine, check with your health care professional.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of cefditoren pivoxil, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular dosing schedule. Do not double doses.

Storage

Store the medicine in a closed container at room temperature, away from heat, moisture, and direct light. Keep from freezing.

Keep out of the reach of children.

Do not keep outdated medicine or medicine no longer needed.

Ask your healthcare professional how you should dispose of any medicine you do not use.

Precautions while using cefditoren pivoxil

If your symptoms do not improve within a few days or if they become worse, check with your doctor.

In some patients, cefditoren may cause diarrhea:

  • Severe diarrhea may be a sign of a serious side effect. Do not take any diarrhea medicine without first checking with your doctor . Diarrhea medicines may make your diarrhea worse or make it last longer.
  • If you have any questions about this or if mild diarrhea continues or gets worse, check with your health care professional.
