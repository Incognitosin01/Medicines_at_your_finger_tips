Uses for Doravirine, Lamivudine, and Tenofovir Disoproxil

Treatment of HIV Infection

Treatment of HIV-1 infection in antiretroviral-naive (have not previously received antiretroviral therapy) or antiretroviral-experienced (previously treated) adults.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>30</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html>

Advice to Patients

  • Critical nature of compliance with HIV therapy and importance of remaining under the care of a clinician.<html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html> Importance of taking as prescribed; do not alter or discontinue antiretroviral regimen without consulting clinician.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html>

  • Antiretroviral therapy is not a cure for HIV infection; opportunistic infections and other complications associated with HIV disease may still occur.<html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html>

  • Advise patients that early initiation of antiretroviral therapy and sustained decreases in plasma HIV RNA have been associated with reduced risk of progression to acquired immunodeficiency syndrome (AIDS) and death.<html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html>

  • Advise patients that effective antiretroviral regimens can decrease HIV levels in blood and genital secretions and strict adherence to such regimens in conjunction with risk-reduction measures may decrease, but cannot absolutely eliminate, the risk of secondary transmission of HIV to others.<html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html> Importance of continuing to practice safer sex (e.g., using latex or polyurethane condoms to minimize sexual contact with body fluids) and reducing high-risk behaviors (e.g., reusing or sharing needles).<html><head></head><body><sup>200</sup></body></html>

  • Importance of reading patient information provided by the manufacturer.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>
