Introduction

Antineoplastic agent; an anti-human epidermal growth factor receptor type 2 (anti-HER2) antibody-drug conjugate consisting of a humanized IgG1 monoclonal antibody (trastuzumab) covalently linked to a type I DNA topoisomerase inhibitor DXd (an exatecan derivative).<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>3</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>4</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>6</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>7</sup></body></html>

Uses for Fam-Trastuzumab Deruxtecan-nxki

Breast Cancer

Treatment of unresectable or metastatic breast cancer in patients whose tumors overexpress the HER2 protein and who have received at least 2 prior anti-HER2-based regimens for metastatic disease.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> <html><head></head><body><sup>2</sup></body></html>

Efficacy determined based on objective response rate and duration of response; clinical benefit not established.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html> Continued approval may be contingent on verification and description of clinical benefit in confirmatory studies.<html><head></head><body><sup>1</sup></body></html>
