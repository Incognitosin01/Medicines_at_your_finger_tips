Description

Henry Schein 104-6308 Bubble Gum Gel

Acclean 60 Second Fluoride Treatment

1.23% APF

16.2 fl oz (480 mL)

NDC 0404-3963-01

A topical anticaries preparation for professional use only.

1.23% fluoride ion from sodium fluoride. Optimized low pH for penetration. Ideal thixotropic action. Artificially flavored.

Directions

Directions:

1. Shake well before using

2. Use after thorough prophylaxis

3. To dispense, fill tray(s) 1/3 with gel

4. Dry teeth completely. Insert tray(s) in mouth and have patient bite down lightly for 60 seconds or up to four minutes for optimal uptake

5. Advise patient not to eat, drink or rinse for 30 minutes after the treatment

Warning

Cautions: Keep out of reach of children.

RX only

Non-Medicinal

Non-Medicinal ingredients: Carboxy methyl cellulose sodium, phosphoric acid, water, red dye, saccharin sodium, titanium dioxide, bubble gum flavor.

Storage

Cautions: Store at room temperature. Do not expose to excessive heat over 40°C, 104°F.

Made in USA
