Use: Labeled Indications

Irrigation: Use as an irrigating fluid for general irrigation, washing, rinsing, and dilution purposes or as an adjunct in the preparation of nutrient mixtures.

Note: For irrigation only; use only for non-intravenous purposes; do NOT administer IV.
