INDICATIONS

INDICATIONS: A mild laxative, antacid for use in ruminant animals with simple indigestion.

DIRECTIONS

DIRECTIONS: Mature bovine, stir one pound of Magnalax Powder into one gallon of water and mix thoroughly.
Administer as a drench or via stomach tube. For sheep, goats and calves administer one pint to one quart
depending on size of animals. Frequent or continued use should be avoided.

Warning

WARNING: Milk that has been taken from animals during treatment and for 12 hours (1 milking)
after the latest treatment must not be used for food.
