Joint Stress

As pets age, they slow down. A homeopathic remedy to provide
temporary relief from: Joint stiffness, soreness, muscle tension,
overexertion, pain in lower back and joint areas.

  • Dosage and Administration

Dosage:
1-20lbs, 5 drops 3 times daily.
21-60lbs, 10 drops 3 times daily.
61-100lbs, 15 drops 3 times daily.
Over 100lbs, 20 drops 3 times daily.
Pets under 1lb, 1-2 drops in water, 3 times daily.
Remedy may be dosed directly into mouth, in water or on food/treat.

When improvement is seen decrease frequency of dosing.
If improvement is maintained stop dosing after 2 days. If symptoms
reappear repeat the original dose.

Visit www.homeopet.com for detailed dosing and information.

Contact Veterinarian if problems persist.
