Product Facts

Each sterile strip is impregnated with 0.5 mg. of fluorexon disodium.

INDICATIONS:

For staining the eye when fitting both soft and hard lenses.

DIRECTIONS FOR USE:

To insure full fluorescence and patient comfort, the Soft Glo impregnated tip should be moistened before application with one or two drops of sterile irrigating or saline solution. Apply moistened tip to conjunctiva or fornix as required. It is recommended that the patient blink several times after application.

1. Hold package in middle and tear down toward sterile strip

2. Turn over and tear down from other side, taking care not to tear the sterile strip.

3. Grasp each end of package firmly as shown, without touching the sterile strip, and pull apart.

NOTE:

For external use only. Contents may not be sterile if the individual strip package has been damaged or previously opened. Keep out of the reach of childred.

HOW SUPPLIED:

Dispenser carton containing 50 strips.
